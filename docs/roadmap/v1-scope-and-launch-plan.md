# Version 1.0 Scope and Launch Plan

## v1.0 Required Scope

Version 1.0 ships the smallest polished career loop:

- Rider/parent onboarding with age, background, bike, and simulation depth.
- One replayable youth season with event-based race planning.
- Season commitment, race-weekend readiness, and explicit go-racing flow.
- Basic race simulation with starts, laps, mistakes, crashes, fatigue, bike condition, results, and memories.
- Basic garage with active bike, stored bikes, wear, parts, provenance, and listing flow.
- Basic marketplace/dealer flow with used listings, search/filter/sort, fitment, negotiation, orders, and delivery.
- Basic family money, stress, trust, age permissions, school/work tradeoffs, and parent approval.
- Minimum memory engine for first race, first win, injury/crash, bike purchase/sale, Loretta attempt, family decision, and season recap.
- Save/load, autosave at week boundaries, and stable serialization of engine state.
- Release readiness: onboarding, UI polish, crash/error logging, performance, privacy/compliance, accessibility/settings, beta plan, and final submission checklist.

## MVP Cutline

### Required for v1.0

- Core career loop from new career to season recap.
- Event-based season planner.
- Age/trust responsibility model.
- Bike-builder and required class-bike transitions.
- Race-weekend readiness and state lifecycle.
- Minimum race simulation.
- Garage, basic marketplace, dealer parts, and money.
- Minimum memory engine and season recap.
- Save/load and release readiness.

### Nice for v1.0

- One Loretta qualifier path if balance and UI clarity are ready.
- Rewarded ads only if fully compliant and non-competitive.
- Cosmetic garage presentation if monetization requires it.
- Expanded accessibility and audio/haptics polish.

### v1.1 Update

- More tracks, class variants, story cards, sponsor depth, travel/camping, education, nutrition, coaching, and contracts.
- Better launch analytics and post-launch event tuning.

### v2.0 Major Update

- Full pro/amateur career expansion.
- Deep world simulation, AI family simulation, dynamic media, weather/surface depth, and career endings.
- Advanced documentary/legacy systems.

### Long-Term Dream Features

- Friends, leaderboards, connected careers, era selection, motocross history engine, and social sharing.
- Memberships, paid expansions, and seasonal collections, only under no-pay-to-win guardrails.

## First Playable Vertical Slice

The first playable slice is one youth season:

1. Create a rider or parent campaign.
2. Choose background and simulation depth.
3. Build an event-based season plan.
4. Review cost/travel/risk and commit.
5. Prepare through weekly choices.
6. Launch race weekends through readiness checks.
7. Simulate races and apply results.
8. Wear and repair the bike.
9. Buy/sell parts or bikes when needed.
10. Generate memories and complete a season recap.

Success criteria:

- The player understands why outcomes happened.
- The season creates at least one memorable family/racing moment.
- The garage and money choices matter.
- The loop is replayable with different backgrounds and plans.

## v1.0 Career Length

Recommended launch length: multiple short youth seasons with simplified class transitions.

Rationale:

- One season proves the loop but can feel too small.
- Full amateur/pro life is too much launch risk.
- Multiple short youth seasons let class moves, bike purchases, memories, and family pressure matter while keeping scope contained.

Deferred:

- Full pro career.
- Full adult life economy.
- Era system.
- Complete amateur-national ecosystem beyond the configurable Loretta path.

## Minimum Launch Definitions

### Race Simulation

Required: starts, lap updates, position changes, fatigue, bike condition, mistakes, crashes/injuries, DNF/DNS, points, standings, and race memories.

Deferred: full track degradation, advanced weather, deep commentary, complex team strategy.

### Garage

Required: active bike, stored bikes, parts, wear, provenance, memories, sell/listing flow, required class-bike transitions.

Deferred: deep museum mode, full property/shop customization.

### Marketplace

Required: used listings, dealer catalog, compatibility, search/filter/sort, buy, sell, negotiation, seller trust basics, orders/delivery.

Deferred: auctions, advanced scams, broad dynamic economy.

### Memory Engine

Required: first race, first win/podium, crash/injury, bike purchase, bike sale, Loretta attempt/qualification, family decision, season recap.

Deferred: full documentary editor and social sharing.

### Monetization

Allowed at launch only if non-competitive and compliant:

- Cosmetics/presentation.
- Restore purchases.
- Rewarded ads that do not alter competitive outcomes.

Forbidden:

- Buying wins, rider stats, better bikes, better parts, race entry, Loretta qualification, injury immunity, sponsor advantages, or hidden outcome boosts.

## Launch Non-Goals

- Full pro career.
- Full documentary engine.
- Full friends/leaderboard/connected-careers stack.
- Era selection and motocross history engine.
- Deep dynamic world and AI family simulation.
- Advanced weather, track surface, travel, camping, and contracts.
- Complete parent campaign beyond the v1 core loop.
- All classes, all regions, all real-world event variants.
- Competitive monetization or pay-to-win shortcuts.

These remain planned roadmap items; they are not deleted or invalidated.
